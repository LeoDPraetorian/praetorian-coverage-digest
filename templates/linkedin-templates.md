# LinkedIn Post Templates - Praetorian Security Inc

Use these templates for the Praetorian company LinkedIn page. Customize the bracketed sections.

## Template 1: External Media Coverage

```
We're honored to be featured in [PUBLICATION] for our work on [TOOL/TOPIC].

[1-2 sentence summary of what the article covers]

[TOOL] is open source and available now: [GITHUB_LINK]

Read the full article: [ARTICLE_URL]

#CyberSecurity #OpenSource #OffensiveSecurity #[TOOL_HASHTAG] #InfoSec
```

We're honored to be featured in Help Net Security for our work on Brutus.
