---

:newspaper: *[PUBLICATION NAME]*

:calendar: [DATE]

## Channel Setup Instructions

### Creating the Channel
1. Create `#praetorian-in-the-wild` as a public channel
2. Set topic: "All external coverage, mentions, and visibility for Praetorian tools and brand"
3. Set purpose: "Automated + manual posts tracking Praetorian mentions across media, GitHub, social, and events"

### Who to Invite
- Marketing team (owners - they process items here)
- Sales team (consumers - they browse for prospect ammo)
- Engineering leads (contributors - they spot coverage marketing misses)
- Leadership (visibility)

### Pinned Message
```
:wave: Welcome to #praetorian-in-the-wild!

This channel tracks ALL external coverage and mentions of Praetorian and our tools.

*How it works:*
:robot_face: Automated monitors post new coverage as they find it
:writing_hand: Team members can manually submit items they spot
:point_right: Marketing reviews and queues items for amplification

*To submit coverage you found:*
Post here with the link and a brief description, or add it to the coverage tracker.

*Status labels:*
:new: New — just discovered
:hourglass_flowing_sand: Queued — scheduled for amplification
:white_check_mark: Amplified — posted to LinkedIn, Slack, website
```
